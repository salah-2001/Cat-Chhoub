The issue of feeding domestic cats is on the minds of most cat breeders
The best healthy food for domestic cats

Cooked Eggs

Cooked eggs are characterized by containing a high percentage of protein, which plays a major role in maintaining the ideal rate of weight for cats, as it works to strengthen their immunity, in addition to containing vitamin B; Which is classified as one of the important vitamins that domestic cats need.

Meat

Meat is the ideal meal for cats, and meat is the main source for cats to obtain food, and meat also contains protein that maintains the health of the cat's heart, eyes, and reproductive system.

Dry Cat Food

Cats need a large amount of vitamins to ensure that they enjoy sound health, as well as to maintain their weight; This is what is available in this type of dry food; As it supplies cats with the vitamins they need for growth, it also gives them complete nutrition, and it is affordable.

Fish

Although it is never recommended to serve raw fish to cats; Because it causes her disease, but all types of dry food for cats; It includes the fish of the ocean, or the sea; Because these fish contain omega-3 fatty acids, which are very beneficial for cats' eyes.

Royal Canine

Domestic cats are accepted to eat this type of dry food; Because it has many advantages, including:
Helps cats control calories; This makes cats maintain an appropriate weight. Stimulating the digestive system because it contains fiber. It contains vitamins and minerals.

Fresh Vegetables

Vegetables are one of the most nutrients that are of great benefit to the health of cats. Because it contains fiber and vitamins, it also stimulates the digestion process, but it must be taken into account that it is well cooked with steam.

Our H e a l t h

Raising an animal, we must take responsibility for maintaining its life and health
Steps to take proper care of cats

Schedule veterinary care

It is important to take your cat to the veterinary clinic regularly, especially as it gets older because the older it gets, the more attention it needs.

Don't ignore her dental health

Teeth are an indicator of its health, and you should make sure to clean the cat's teeth and mouth periodically at the veterinarian, and clean them at home regularly.

Notice the cat's behavioral changes

You should notice whether the cat is behaving normally or not, often when it is exposed to a health problem, this is reflected in its behavior significantly

Don't give her a lot of food

Some believe that putting a large amount of food for the cat is a kind of pampering and extra care, but this is a big mistake and may reflect negatively on it and harm it.

Don't neglect exercise

Cats also need exercise, like dogs, to ensure they stay healthy, so you should make sure that you exercise your cat regularly to help it build and develop its muscles and keep its bones strong and healthy.

Water saving and easy access

It is recommended to put the water in a clean, small bowl and replace it daily, so that the water attracts the cat to drink, and keep the cat's water away from the litter box and food.
